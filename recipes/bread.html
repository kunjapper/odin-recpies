Mom's Famous Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • Baguettes
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Place a garlic bulb covered in olive oil and sprinkled with salt and pepper into a foil sheet and wrap. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until roasted brown.
  2. Mix roasted garlic and butter in a bowl. Spread on to slices of bread.
  3. Bake at 350 for 10 minutes until golden brown.
